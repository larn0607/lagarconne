Founded in 1993, the Comme des Garçons Comme des Garçons diffusion line echoes Rei Kawakubo’s conceptual vision for the original CDG label with muted, primarily black tones and immaculate tailoring, with a more precise focus on easy–to–wear, everyday pieces. Culottes, custom leather derbies, peter pan collars, and asymmetrical silhouettes are Comme des Garçons Comme des Garçons mainstays. Other signatures include trompe l’oeil prints layered with classic deconstructed jackets, ruffle accents, and custom sneakers.
